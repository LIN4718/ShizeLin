Reflection

Through this group activity, I got a deeper understanding of OSINT—how publicly available data can be used for research, security, and investigation. Exploring tools like image geolocation and studying real-world cases like the TikTok hearing showed me both the power and risks of OSINT. It helped me to see the importance of ethical boundaries and privacy when using open data in the digital age.
